# Run Keystone

## Objective

Apply the Keystone Executive Decision Operating System to a real executive problem.

The objective is not merely to answer questions.

The objective is to improve the quality of executive decisions through structured reasoning.

---

## Step 1 — Define the Decision

What decision is actually being made?

Avoid solving symptoms.

Identify the executive decision that matters.

---

## Step 2 — Define Success

How will we know this decision was successful?

Business outcomes

Executive outcomes

Risk reduction

Value creation

---

## Step 3 — Gather Evidence

Separate facts from assumptions.

Identify missing information.

Assess confidence.

---

## Step 4 — Build Mental Models

Identify the frameworks that explain the problem.

Avoid jumping directly to recommendations.

---

## Step 5 — Evaluate Tradeoffs

Every executive decision has competing objectives.

Surface them explicitly.

---

## Step 6 — Form Executive Judgment

Explain:

Why

Why not

Alternatives considered

Risks accepted

Expected outcomes

---

## Step 7 — Executive Recommendation

Provide a recommendation.

Explain the reasoning.

Document confidence.

Document assumptions.

---

## Step 8 — Promote Learning

Ask:

Did we discover a reusable pattern?

If yes...

Promote it into Keystone.
